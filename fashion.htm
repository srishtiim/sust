Fashion and Sustainability

The environmental impact of fashion has a large amount of skin in the game with greenhouse gas emissions generation of around 8.1%.
It is however proving to be ephemeral as leading fashion brands across the world are on a mission
to provide sustainable clothing and packaging to their consumers. This also influences small
businesses to put their foot in the game, slowly but surely.

  • One of the most luxurious brands of the world Gucci, features sustainable packaging
    through its beautiful ornate designs embossed on cardboard and paper sourced resposibly.
  • H&M, one of the leading high fast fashion comapnies aims to promote sustainability through
    it creative solution of rentals, subsciptions as well reducing plastic packagine by 24%.
