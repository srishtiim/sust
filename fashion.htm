Fashion and Sustainability

Fashion is one of the leading players in the industry. And who doesn't love it? However the environmental impact of fashion has a large amount of skin in the game with greenhouse gas emissions generation of around 8.1%.
It is however proving to be ephemeral as leading fashion brands across the world are on a mission to provide sustainable clothing and packaging to their consumers.
This also influences small businesses to put their foot in the game, slowly but surely.

  • One of the most luxurious brands of the world Gucci, features sustainable packaging
    through its beautiful ornate designs embossed on cardboard and paper sourced resposibly.

  • H&M , one of the leading high fast fashion comapnies aims to promote sustainability through
    it creative solution of rentals, subsciptions as well reducing plastic packagine by 24%.It also started the H&M conscious
    collection where materials like organic cotton are used.

  • Levi's is changing the denim game with its new collection which uses upto 96% less water to
    produce its clothes and recycles more than 20% of water used. It is committed to its strategy of maximizing water efficiency.

  • Another big brand like Ralph Lauren is making a simple change which could create an immense impact.
    This is done by the brand by recycling plastic bottles to create its pieces in the collection "The Earth Polo".
